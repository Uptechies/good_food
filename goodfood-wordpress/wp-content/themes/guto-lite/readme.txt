== Description ==

Guto Lite is a fast, fully customizable & beautiful WordPress theme suitable for your Startup, IT Agency, Fashion Shop, Business Startup, and blog websites. It has the WooCommerce shopping feature that included making your online shopping experience more comfortable and smarter. It is lightweight and offers exceptional speed to keep your website visitors engaged! 

The theme is SEO optimized, Responsive, W3 valid, and compatible with browsers such as IE11, Edge, Chrome, Brave, Firefox, Safari, Opera, UC, etc. The theme demo is built with Elementor page builder. 

If you like our work and find the theme useful for your project, please consider a 5-star review with your feedback! That will inspire us a lot to add more exciting new demos in Guto Lite.

. Demo: https://demo.gutotheme.com/

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in GutoTheme in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.
4. Navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel and customize to taste.
